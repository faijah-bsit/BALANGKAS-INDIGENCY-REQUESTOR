Welcome!

Welcome to CertiGo, your trusted online platform for requesting barangay indigency certificates. Our goal is to make community services more accessible by bringing the process online — simple, fast, and convenient for every resident.


What is a Certificate of Indigency?

A Certificate of Indigency is an official document issued by the barangay that proves a person or family has low or insufficient income. It is usually requested to avail of government programs, scholarships, medical or financial assistance, and other benefits for those who are considered indigent.
